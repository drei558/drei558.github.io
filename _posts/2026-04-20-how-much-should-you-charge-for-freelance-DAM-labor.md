How much should you charge for digital asset manager and data freelance labor? Your rates are your rates. Only you know your finances. But here's some ideas on what to charge based on day rates of freelancers in creative industries. 

Freelance/independent contract work doesn't necessarily fit neatly into a box. You will likely be doing labor traditionally done by multiple job roles - so charge accordingly.

[Major Players surveyed creatives in the UK](https://www.majorplayers.co/en/insights/creative-industries-market-intelligence-report-q1) and asked about compensation, skills, and AI use. Some interesting stats that feel applicable: 

1. the average day rate of freelancers was £407 (~ $551 USD/day or $68/hour)
2. 61% saw no movement in their day rate over the past year
3. Creativity and innovation, critical thinking and emotional intelligence are the most cited critical soft skills across the workforce - with freelancers rating creativity highest of any group at 54%.
4. Nearly half of all freelancers have 8+ years of experience. "In a market where clients are prioritizing proven capability, emerging talent faces the same barrier it does in permanent hiring: without experience, you can't get the work - and without the work, you can't get the experience."
